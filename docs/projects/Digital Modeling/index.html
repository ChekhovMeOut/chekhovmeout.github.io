Background

I would not consider myself an accomplished artist by any stretch of the imagination - but that doesn't mean I can't enjoy the pursuit! I was exposed to CAD software for architecture in high school and enjoyed it thoroughly. I found myself trying to make art, designs, and textures in Onshape. I eventually discovered Blender during the coronavirus lockdown and used my newfound free time to begin learning the software. Similarly, I was inspired by unit operations to create the vector art background of this site in Adobe Illustrator.

I am nowhere close to producing at a professional level, but I find it to be an engaging and fun hobby. The images here are rendered from projects, tutorials, and scratch work I've made so far. I plan to collaborate with peers by producing models they can integrate into their own games and projects.
